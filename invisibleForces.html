Invisible Forces

(Student Work)

Interacting with another person has various effects on oneself. Some of these are quite visible for everyone to see, others however remain hidden deep inside. Invisible Forces is an installation, created in collaboration with Menno van den Berg, which attempts to visualize these veiled feelings.

The installation consists of a luminescent animatronic blob like creature, laying dormant. Attached to this creature are two mind-flex (EEG) devices which measure the intensity of brainwaves. The brain wave data from EEGs is then used to bring this creature to life. It reacts in the form of movement and subtle glows in order to visualize the data and showcase the effect these two users have on each other.

Made with Arduino.
