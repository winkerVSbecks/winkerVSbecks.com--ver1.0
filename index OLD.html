Varun Vachhar is currently a student of Interactive/Media/Design program at the Royal Academy of Arts, the Hague. He is a designer/technologist who is interested in everything from art to engineering. Previously he graduated from the University of Alberta (Edmonton, Canada) with a B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering. Varun has a deep curiosity for all forms of design and making. His engineering education has engrained within him a strong sense of curosity with an emphasis on the pursuit of creative design solutions. He is interested in interaction design, generative visuals, graphic design, data visualization, algorithmic art, software, immersive environments, new media, electronics/hacking, 2D/3D animation, motion design, scripting, industrial design and photography.
