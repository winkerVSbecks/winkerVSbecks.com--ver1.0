The OVNA Project

(Personal Project)

In 2011 the Dutch public transport system was revamped to use a RFID based payment system. Due to the nature of RFID, each person has an unique alphanumeric ID embedded in their card and it always stays the same. The commuters have to scan their card every time they get on or off a bus/tram/train/metro/boat/etc and each time this ID of theirs is logged. My goal was to make people aware of this and visualize their digital identity.
