Attracted

(Mark Ridder/PICNIC/WCIT)

This is an installation I built together with Dutch artist Mark Ridder. It focuses on the interaction of the user with a graphic grid. A translucent screen is filled with one wall of colour, when someone walks across it, it explodes into smaller pieces. These pieces, stick together to form the 'shadow' of the person standing right across.

It was commisioned by PICNIC for their event, An Amsterdam PICNIC @ WCIT 2010.

Made with Processing.
